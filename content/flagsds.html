--- title: All flags and pennants ---

Main design

Our goal was to design flags and pennants that look like the ones we use for running sailing races. The designs therefore have an outline so that all images stand out on a white background, and the colours are chosen to be easy to distinguish whilst looking natural on the screen and in print, including a slight grey shade to 'white' elements.
